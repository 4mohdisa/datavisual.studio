# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that helps: being respectful of differing viewpoints, giving and gracefully
accepting constructive feedback, and focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct which could
reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at
**mohdisa233@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a
response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The maintainer is obligated
to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
